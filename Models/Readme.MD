# **UwU30 case models**

### There are 2 options available for this case:
1. A simplified case which uses less supports at the cost of losing visual features. The typing experience is exactly the same
2. A more complex version which has multicolor and requires *many* supports. Using something like Bambu's PLA support material as the interface layer is HIGHLY encouraged


### For hardware you will need:
- [8 M3x6 screws](https://a.co/d/03mqxs5H)
- [8 M3 heatset inserts](https://a.co/d/09Gc49wN)
- [6 SKUF feet](https://keeb.io/products/skuf-silicone-rubber-keyboard-feet)
- [4 tadpoles](https://saberkeebs.com/products/geon-tadpoles)

### Notes
- I have put a 0.25mm slit around most of the perimiter of the case. I did this to make it so hull lines will not develop and the print will look best
- I am still new to case design so there may be small issues/less than ideal design choices
- For the more complex multicolor version, the sections to make other colors may be a bit hard to select and paint in Bambu Studio, but it is possible
- The thick plate is for both case designs
- The badge stays in with press fit on my case, but you may need a bit of glue to ensure it stays in place
